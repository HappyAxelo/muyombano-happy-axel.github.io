About

I am a Research Assistant at the Transformative Artificial Intelligence Research and Innovation Lab at the University of Rwanda, where I completed my MSc in Embedded Computer Systems with concentrations in Artificial Intelligence and Internet of Things.

I am passionate about technology-driven impact on social lives. With over 4 years of diverse experience through internships, volunteering, and engineering roles, I bring a combination of technical knowledge, project management, and business analysis skills. I also serve as Managing Director at Ampere Vision Rwanda Ltd, focusing on AI solutions and product development.

My work includes developing AI-powered solutions for social impact, such as "Shisha Mwana" - an AI mobile-based malnutrition early detection and prevention system in collaboration with NCST & RBC.
